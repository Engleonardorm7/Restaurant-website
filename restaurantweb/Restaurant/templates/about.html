About My Restaurant

My Restaurant: A Taste of Colombia Right Here

My Restaurant isn't just a restaurant; it's a fiesta for your senses! Step through our vibrant doors and be transported to the heart of Colombia, where colorful decorations, energetic music, and the irresistible aroma of sizzling fajitas fill the air.

Founded by the Rodriguez family in 2014, My Restaurant has been a cornerstone of the local community for generations. Our passion lies in sharing the authentic flavors and traditions of our heritage, using only the freshest ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

A Fiesta for Your Palate:

Our menu is a vibrant tapestry of Colombian classics, prepared with love and expertise. From melt-in-your-mouth carne asada to sizzling fajitas, savory mole poblano, and fresh seafood dishes bursting with coastal flavors, we cater to every craving. Our homemade tortillas, crafted daily with love, offer the perfect accompaniment to each dish.

More Than Just Food:

At My Restaurant, we believe in creating a truly immersive experience. Our lively atmosphere is infectious, with energetic music filling the air, friendly staff dressed in traditional clothing, and vibrant decorations that transport you to a Colombian fiesta. We host regular live music nights featuring talented local musicians, adding another layer of joy to your dining experience.

Making Memories that Last:

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, enjoying a casual meal with friends, or simply seeking a taste of Colombia, My Restaurant is the perfect destination. We cater to all occasions, offering spacious seating for large groups, intimate corners for romantic dinners, and a festive bar area for lively gatherings.

Come join us at My Restaurant and experience the warmth of Colombian hospitality, the vibrancy of our culture, and the unforgettable flavors of our cuisine. We promise a fiesta for your senses that will leave you wanting more!
